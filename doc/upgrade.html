How to upgrade s6-portable-utils

from 1.0.0 to 1.0.1

1.0.1 is a bugfix release. No changes in dependencies.

from 0.17 to 1.0.0

Release 1.3.0 of skalibs is needed to build and/or run this version of s6-portable-utils.

FHS support has been added. See the end of this page for the rationale, and the general skarnet.org configuration page for how to proceed.

from 0.16 to 0.17

No change in dependencies (bugfix release).

It is recommended, as usual, to always switch to the latest version of skalibs.

from 0.15 to 0.16

No change in dependencies (cleanup release).

from 0.14 to 0.15

No change in dependencies (bugfix release).

from 0.13 to 0.14

No change in dependencies. It is recommended to use skalibs version 1.2.4 or higher to benefit from general bugfixes and enhancements.

from 0.12 to 0.13

No change in dependencies. It is recommended to use skalibs version 1.2.3 or higher to benefit from general bugfixes and enhancements.

from 0.11 to 0.12

No change in dependencies. It is recommended to use skalibs version 1.2.1 or higher to benefit from general bugfixes and enhancements.

from 0.10 to 0.11

No change in dependencies. It is recommended to use skalibs version 1.1.1 or higher to benefit from general bugfixes and enhancements.
